What is Infantino?

Infantino is a tech competition organised annually by the computer science students of St. Joseph's School. Greater Noida. The main purpose of the competition is to explore the tech genius hidden inside the students by conducting various tech based events like debugging, game making, robotics & many more.
The event has been a proud legacy for the past seven years, with its current iteration titled Mark 7. Spanning one to two days, it serves as a platform that celebrates some of the most brilliant and exceptional minds in technology.
